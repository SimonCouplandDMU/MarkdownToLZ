## Math

Inline math: $E = mc^2$

Display math:

$$
\int_0^1 x^2 dx
$$

### Example

#### Quadratic Formula

To solve a quadratic equation:

$$
ax^2 + bx + c = 0
$$

Use the formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

Where:
- $a$, $b$, and $c$ are constants
- $\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}$ is called the discriminant
